Our Academic Programs

Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Comprehensive undergraduate program in liberal arts fostering critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural awareness.

  • Multiple specialization options
  • Research methodology training
  • Seminar presentations
  • Cultural activities participation

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.)

Professional commerce education covering accounting, finance, taxation, and business fundamentals for career readiness.

  • Practical accounting training
  • Industry-relevant curriculum
  • Computer application skills
  • Internship opportunities

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

Rigorous science education with hands-on laboratory experience in various scientific disciplines and research methods.

  • Well-equipped laboratories
  • Practical experiments
  • Scientific research projects
  • Field study opportunities

Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A.)

Comprehensive computer science program preparing students for careers in software development and IT industry.

  • Programming languages training
  • Software development projects
  • Database management
  • Industry internships

M.A. Hindi Literature

Advanced study of Hindi literature, poetry, prose, and literary criticism with research opportunities in contemporary themes.

  • Classical & modern literature
  • Research dissertation
  • Literary criticism training
  • Publication opportunities

M.A. Geography

Comprehensive geography program covering physical, human, and environmental geography with fieldwork and research components.

  • Field survey techniques
  • GIS and mapping skills
  • Environmental studies
  • Research methodology
